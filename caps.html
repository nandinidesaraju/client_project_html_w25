Counseling and Psychological Services (CAPS)

Welcome to CAPS

Your mental health and well-being are essential for your success. CAPS provides a variety of resources to support you throughout your journey at the University of Michigan.

Services Offered

  • Individual Counseling: Meet one-on-one with a licensed counselor to address personal, academic, or professional challenges.
  • Group Therapy: Join support groups to connect with peers facing similar challenges.
  • Workshops and Seminars: Participate in sessions focusing on stress management, mindfulness, and more.
  • 24/7 Crisis Support: Access immediate support in times of crisis through the CAPS crisis hotline.

How to Get Started

To make an appointment or learn more about CAPS, visit the CAPS website, call (734) 764-8312, or stop by the office during walk-in hours.

Office Locations

CAPS has offices in multiple locations to make accessing services easier for students:

  • Central Campus: Michigan Union, Room 3100
  • North Campus: Pierpont Commons, Room 2301
  • Virtual Services: Available via Zoom for remote support
