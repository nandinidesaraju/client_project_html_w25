Counseling and Psychological Services (CAPS)

Welcome to CAPS

Your mental health and well-being are essential for your success at the University of Michigan. CAPS provides a wide range of resources designed to support your emotional, psychological, and academic well-being. Whether you are feeling overwhelmed by coursework, experiencing personal challenges, or simply need someone to talk to, CAPS is here to help.

We recognize that every student’s journey is unique, and our services are tailored to meet diverse needs. From confidential one-on-one counseling to group therapy and skill-building workshops, our team is dedicated to fostering a supportive and understanding environment. Seeking help is a sign of strength, and our goal is to provide the tools and resources you need to thrive both academically and personally.

Services Offered

  • Individual Counseling: Meet one-on-one with a licensed counselor to discuss personal, academic, or professional concerns in a confidential setting. These sessions can help you navigate stress, relationships, identity exploration, and other challenges you may be facing.
  • Group Therapy: Join support groups where students with shared experiences can connect, learn from one another, and receive guidance from trained professionals. Group therapy provides a sense of community and belonging, helping students feel less alone in their struggles.
  • Workshops and Seminars: Participate in interactive sessions focused on stress management, mindfulness, coping strategies, and personal growth. These workshops provide valuable tools to enhance resilience and well-being.
  • 24/7 Crisis Support: Immediate assistance is available through the CAPS crisis hotline for students experiencing emotional distress or urgent mental health concerns. This service ensures that help is accessible whenever you need it, day or night.
  • Outreach Programs: CAPS also works with student organizations and faculty to provide mental health education, training, and outreach initiatives that promote a culture of wellness across campus.

How to Get Started

Taking the first step toward seeking support can feel intimidating, but CAPS makes the process simple and welcoming. Students can schedule an appointment to meet with a counselor, attend a drop-in consultation, or access self-help resources online.

To schedule an appointment, visit the CAPS website, call (734) 764-8312, or stop by the office during walk-in hours. If you are experiencing a crisis or need immediate support, 24/7 assistance is available by calling the crisis support line.

Office Locations

CAPS has multiple locations across campus to make accessing services more convenient for students. Whether you prefer in-person appointments or virtual consultations, we strive to make support accessible and flexible for your needs.

  • Central Campus: Michigan Union, Room 3100 – Ideal for students living in or attending classes on Central Campus.
  • North Campus: Pierpont Commons, Room 2301 – A convenient location for students in engineering, music, and other North Campus programs.
  • Virtual Services: Online counseling sessions are available via Zoom for students who prefer remote support.

No matter where you are, you have access to CAPS resources. Our goal is to make sure all students can find the support they need, when and where they need it.
