BSI Academic Advising

Welcome to BSI Academic Advising

The BSI Academic Advising team is here to support you throughout your journey at the School of Information. Our goal is to ensure your academic and professional success by providing guidance, resources, and opportunities tailored to your needs.

Services Offered

  • Academic Planning: Work with advisors to create a personalized academic plan that aligns with your goals.
  • Course Selection: Get assistance in choosing the right courses to meet your degree requirements and interests.
  • Career Guidance: Explore career options and receive advice on internships, job opportunities, and networking.
  • Workshops: Participate in skill-building workshops on time management, stress reduction, and more.

Contact BSI Academic Advising

To learn more about the services provided by BSI Academic Advising or to schedule an appointment, visit the BSI Academic Advising Website.
