BSI Academic Advising

Welcome to BSI Academic Advising

The BSI Academic Advising team is here to support you throughout your journey at the School of Information. Whether you need guidance on course selection, career planning, or academic resources, our advisors are dedicated to helping you navigate your undergraduate experience successfully. We aim to provide a supportive and inclusive environment where students can feel confident in their academic and professional goals.

Our advisors offer personalized one-on-one sessions where you can discuss academic concerns, degree progress, or future aspirations. We also provide workshops, networking opportunities, and referrals to campus resources to ensure you have a well-rounded experience. No matter where you are in your academic journey, we are here to help you succeed!

Services Offered

  • Academic Planning: Work with advisors to create a personalized academic plan that aligns with your goals. Whether you’re exploring different specializations or planning your coursework for future semesters, we can help you map out a clear and achievable path.
  • Course Selection: Get assistance in choosing the right courses to meet your degree requirements and interests. Our advisors can help you understand prerequisites, elective options, and how to balance your workload efficiently.
  • Career Guidance: Explore career options and receive advice on internships, job opportunities, and networking. From resume reviews to interview preparation, we provide the resources you need to transition from student to professional.
  • Workshops: Participate in skill-building workshops on time management, stress reduction, and more. These workshops are designed to help you develop practical skills that contribute to both academic success and personal well-being.
  • Graduate School Preparation: If you're considering further education, we can provide guidance on graduate school applications, program selection, and preparing strong application materials.

Contact BSI Academic Advising

If you have questions about your academic progress, need help making decisions about your coursework, or simply want to check in about your long-term goals, our advisors are here to assist you. You can schedule an appointment with an academic advisor or attend drop-in advising hours for quick questions and immediate support.

To learn more about the services provided by BSI Academic Advising or to schedule an appointment, visit the BSI Academic Advising Website.

Office Location

The BSI Academic Advising Office is conveniently located in North Quad, Suite 2600. Students are encouraged to visit during office hours to speak with an advisor, ask questions, or get information on upcoming events and workshops. We strive to create a welcoming space where students can access the resources they need to thrive academically and professionally.

Our office hours are Monday–Friday, 9:00 AM–5:00 PM. Whether you prefer to schedule a one-on-one appointment or drop in for a quick conversation, we are happy to assist you with any academic or career-related concerns.
